About Me

I was born in Orange, Southern California, and moved to Fountain Valley (which was 10 miles away) when I was 3. I was raised in Orange County my whole life. Both my parents were born in Vietnam, and moved to the U.S. in their teens. I have a younger sister, and also a dog named Wilson. I've been playing tennis pretty much my whole life, and now it's become my career. When I was a kid I played the piano, and now that I'm older I'm itching to go back to relearning how to play music. I enjoy being outside, connecting with nature, going for walks, taking everything in, as much as I can, as often as I can. I love experiencing new things, seeing new sights, it's a thrill. When I'm older I would love to take a year and travel and see everything the world has to offer.

Places I want to visit and what I want to do in them:

  1. Banff National Park, Canada
    • To experience being in the Canadian Rockies
  2. Lauterbrunnen, Switzerland
    • To see the Swiss Alps and Alpine Lakes
  3. Norway
    • To see the Northern Lights
  4. Dolomites, Italy
    • To hike the mountains
  5. Pacific Coast Hwy
    • One of the most iconic roads, with the most picturesque sights
  6. Alaska
    • To experience 24/7 daylight in the summer of course!
